aed aims to revolutionize cargo transportation in space, allowing fast access to orbits which are otherwise expensive and slow to reach through the provision of simple, secure and fast in-space transportation services. We envision a future where moving around in space becomes as common as doing it on Earth. This is a future where our space “taxies” are safe, agile, low-cost, and often thrilling – able to move cargo, and eventually people, to multiple outposts. Our aim is to radically increase the safety and reduce the cost of in-space transportation by applying matured and innovating propulsion technologies to reach further and faster. aed aims to provision the means so everyone can do extraordinary things – making them ordinary.

CIRCUIT

Recurrent point-to-point service of in-space transportation to cargo will be provided. Starting with small payloads (instruments and nanosatellites), aed aims to solve the “last mile“ problem with varied and customizable in-orbit transportation services, empowering everyone with the capabilities to reach further. By using our delivery vehicle, circuits to multiple orbital destinations will be offered, allowing the users to know where they could go, and when - just like train and bus lines on Earth.

DEDICATED

Through aed, the space community will also have the opportunity to conduct customized independent missions at a fraction of the cost of old-fashioned space programs. From providing Earth-orbit changes to unlocking fast and affordable deep space research, expanding the applicability of small satellites by getting to hand-picked orbits which are otherwise of difficult reach.
